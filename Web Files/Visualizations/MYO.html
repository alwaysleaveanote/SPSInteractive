Make Your Own!


Overview

This tools allows you to compare different types of data side by side. Simply choose how many charts (in the form of bar graphs) and maps you want to display, then choose what information should be displayed on each. For example, if you wanted to see how rates of free and reduced priced lunch compare with average test scores, choose 0 charts and 2 maps, color one by free and reduced priced lunch and the other by overall tests scores.

Note that this visualization may be slow to load, but it will work.
